A Century of Innovation

Founded in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines, BMW has evolved into a global symbol of performance, engineering excellence, and automotive luxury. Our journey began with the iconic Rapp Motorenwerke and blossomed into the global powerhouse we are today.

Driving for a Greener Tomorrow

By 2030, at least half of the BMW Group's global sales are to consist of all-electric vehicles. We are committed to a circular economy, significantly reducing our carbon footprint across the entire lifecycle of our vehicles.

Our 2030 Goals

  • Reduction of CO2 emissions by 40% per vehicle.
  • Use of 50% recycled materials in vehicle production.
  • 100% green electricity for all production sites worldwide.
